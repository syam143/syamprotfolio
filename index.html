Who I Am

About Me

PSY

I am an academician, researcher, and mentor with a passion for uncovering the intersection of technology and human behavior in business contexts.

Currently serving as Assistant Professor in the School of Business and Management at CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bengaluru, I bring a rich blend of industry insight and scholarly rigor to every classroom and research project.

My doctoral research (PhD, 2022) in Business Management laid the foundation for exploring how digital intelligence, artificial intelligence, and consumer psychology intersect in modern markets. I have guided doctoral scholars, presented at international conferences, and contributed to faculty development programs across India.
